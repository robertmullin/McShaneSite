About Me

I have been a freelance Production Designer/Art Director for 4 years. For over 10 years at The Canadian Broadcasting Corporation as a Production Designer/Art Director . My experience includes flexible time management, staffing and delegation of duties, including the supervision of all trades. Experience in both scripted and factual production. Throughout my career I have worked diligently to interpret the creative vision of each project while also successfully developing and managing accurate budgets and production timelines.

Design Experience

From project inception to completion in varied television production genres, special event projects, office and residential design. Administration and Communication: Provide administrative support for a technical staff. Organize work schedules to meet strict deadlines. Establish and maintain strong working relationships with all levels of production. Strengths and Accomplishments: Enthusiastic and creative with demonstrated ability to meet deadlines and objectives. Excellent organizational and communication talents; solid skills in improving productivity and efficiency to reduce costs.
