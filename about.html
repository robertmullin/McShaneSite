About Me

I have been a freelance Production Designer/Art Director for 4 years. For over 10 years at The Canadian Broadcasting Corporation as a Production Designer/Art Director . My experience includes flexible time management, staffing and delegation of duties, including the supervision of all trades. Experience in both scripted and factual production. Throughout my career I have worked diligently to interpret the creative vision of each project while also successfully developing and managing accurate budgets and production timelines.
